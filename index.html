LUCID

LUCID, a pet project, is a health tracker of Bipolar Patients. While learning about this illness, I realised that Bipolar Patients were often unaware of their plight which makes it harder for them to cope with their illness.
Role in this project: UX Research, Ideation, UI Design & Branding

Optimising iTines

In this project, I redesigned iTunes for Windows. I deconstructed the desktop app to it's most crucial components - organising and listening to music, and rebuilt to better suit its purposes.
Processes: Usability testing, User Flows, Information Architecture

Axis Bank Designathon

In this article, I write about my experience at the Axis Bank Designathon. I highlight our thought process and the experience as a whole.
Outcome: Fourth Place

UnPixelate

We hope to create an Indian community for design by bringing in the best designers of India on a podcast series to talk about their principles and experiences within this field. Over the last 2 months, we have gotten designers from Apple, Amazon and Flipkart on board as our speakers.

minimise

A pet project in collaboration with Aabharan Hemanth to promote sustainable design by using innovative process to combat production waste.
